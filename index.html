⚡ Quick Presets
🎯 Keep Quality on TikTok, YouTube & Instagram
📐
Resolution & bitrate

Upload at 50 Mbps minimum. Platforms re-compress everything — starting higher means less detail lost after their compression pass.

TikTok: 1080×1920YouTube: 1920×1080

🔪
Always pre-sharpen

Every platform applies a blur pass when compressing. Use the 🔪 Sharpen chip before export — it runs a real GPU unsharp mask to compensate.

Critical for TikTokRecommended for YT

🌈
Pre-boost contrast & colour

Compression flattens colour and mutes contrast. Add +10–20 Contrast and +10–15 Saturation so it looks natural after their encoder.

Use TikTok Ready preset →

💎
The 4K YouTube trick

YouTube gives 4K uploads a better codec (VP9/AV1) even when viewed at 1080p. Use 💎 4K Ultra Crisp preset for noticeably sharper streams.

YouTube 4K trick

⏱️
Keep your native frame rate

Don't convert 24fps to 30fps or vice versa — it causes judder. TikTok handles 30fps best. YouTube handles both 30 and 60fps well.

TikTok: 30fps idealYT: 30 or 60fps

📦
MP4 H.264 is always safest

Every platform's ingest pipeline is optimised for H.264 MP4. Other formats get transcoded an extra time, losing quality. SmartVideo exports MP4 by default.

MP4 H.264 ✓TikTok ✓YouTube ✓
